A Word From Our New Patron

We are pleased to announce that we have a new patron who will be supporting BWCR and the work we do re-homing cats.

Councillor Rikki Teml is Chairman of Filton Town Council, and a big supporter of BWCR:

“I am delighted to have been asked to be a patron, I have seen first hand the great work Bristol & Wales Cat Rescue carry out through adopting two cats along with volunteering as a home checker myself. I look forward to continuing to support this fantastic charity operated by a dedicated and professional team of volunteers.”

Our Shop

Did you know, BWCR has a charity shop?

Drop in and say hello! All proceeds from the shop go towards funding the vital work we do, caring for and rehoming unwanted or neglected cats and kittens.

Volunteer or Support

We’re always looking for volunteers to help man the shop. If you can spare some time on a regular basis (even just a few hours a week), please get in touch.

Donations to the shop are also appreciated. Please feel free to drop by with your (good quality) clothing, knick-knacks, jewellery, homeware, books, CDs, DVDs and soft furnishings.
